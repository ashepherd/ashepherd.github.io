I'm a terrible writer. I don't practice enough, and proposal writing freaks me out. I survive by making a playlist each time that helps me focus.
  • Stayin' Alive, Scary Pockets, Lizzy McAlpine - Infinite Game Theory suggests the #1 objective of all business enterprises should be to continue to play the game. The proposal process is all about sticking around one more day.
  • Shake It Up, The Breathing Effect - "What's it gonna be today? I guess I'll do the same thing....as yesterday."
  • Bad Habit, Steve Lacy - Is what we are writing going to land with the reviewers? Steve Lacy catpures it...."I wish I knew you wanted me!"
  • Bad Idea, Cordae - I'm gonna write to terrible stuff. "Looking at the bigger picture, portrait, and I smile wide."
  • People Under the Stairs, Mac Miller - This song was released during an epic promotion by Mac Miller. The story of how he built an audience is inspiring "...I'm locked in the lab, workin'"
